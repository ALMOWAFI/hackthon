Privacy Policy

Academic Document Analyzer

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how the Academic Document Analyzer ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your data, especially when processing sensitive academic documents.

Information We Collect

When you use our Academic Document Analyzer, we collect the following types of information:

  • Uploaded Documents: Math exams, academic assessments, and other documents you upload for analysis.
  • Processing Data: Information generated during the analysis process, including detected answers, error markings, and feedback.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our service, such as timestamps of uploads and analysis requests.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Document Analysis: To process and analyze your academic documents using our OCR and assessment tools.
  • Service Improvement: To improve our analysis algorithms, error detection, and feedback generation.
  • User Support: To provide technical support and respond to your inquiries.

Local Processing & Data Security

We prioritize your privacy and data security through the following measures:

  • Local Processing: Documents are processed locally on your device whenever possible, minimizing data transmission.
  • Temporary Storage: Uploaded documents are stored only for the duration needed to complete the analysis.
  • Secure Transmission: When data must be transmitted, we use secure, encrypted connections.
  • No Unnecessary Retention: We do not store your documents or analysis results beyond what is necessary to provide the service.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Your documents and analysis results are kept strictly confidential.

We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect and defend our rights and property

Educational Institution Policy

If you are using this service as part of an educational institution:

  • We adhere to educational privacy standards and regulations.
  • Student information is treated with additional care and confidentiality.
  • Analysis results may be made available to authorized educators as specified by your institution's policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of future communications
  • Request a copy of your analysis results

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date.
